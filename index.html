C O L O R A D O

Colorado is a state in the western region of the United States that joined the union as the 38th state in 1876. Colorado has plenty of things to do and is a very good vacation destination.





Good Hotels in Colorado






The Lodge at Breckenridge

The Lodge at Breckenridge is the perfect place for your Rocky Mountain getaway! Nestled on a forested cliff at 10,200 feet, our newly renovated 57-room lodge offers rustic Colorado charm with spectacular million-dollar views of Boreas Pass, Breckenridge Ski Resort, and the Tenmile Range.
